1. Create Accounts

  You can create your account in Stage Candlepin (subscription.rhn.stage.redhat.com) on this tab. It's optional to entitle the account with Subscription SKUs and Quantity which can be added after account creation on Entitle the account tab. You can test your account via the Stage Customer Portal.

  NOTE: You can check your account before creation via View Accounts tab.

2. Add Subscription Pools

  You can populate your account on this tab by adding pools of subscriptions. These pools will appear in the list of all available subscriptions to systems that have registered to this account.

  NOTE: You can check your account before creation via View Accounts tab.

3. Create Accounts from CSV

  You can create and entitle your accounts from the uploaded csv file on this tab.

4. Search Subscription SKUs

   Search by Product: Select the product name, and click 'Search' button, then the 'SKU' column is just the corresponding Subscription SKUs.

   Search by SKU Name: Fill in the SKU Name, you can input 1 or more SKU Name separated by comma, and click 'Search' button, then the 'SKU' column is just the corresponding Subscription SKUs.

  Advanced Search:

    Search by SKU Attributes: Select the SKU attribute, then select the search method that you want to use, after that fill in the attribute value, you need to input at least 1 SKU attribute and the largest number is 5 attributes, and click 'Search' button, then the 'SKU' column is just the corresponding Subscription SKUs.

  NOTE: All the Subscription SKUs are all from Stage Candlepin, not Production Candlepin.

5. Manage Accounts

  View Accounts: You can view your account in CSV format on this tab, please input the username and password.

  Export Accounts: You can export your accounts on this tab.

    NOTE: The supported browsers include Firefox(20+), Chrome, IE(10+), Opera(15+).

  Refresh Accounts: You can refresh the Subscriptions of your account on this tab.

6. Known Issues

   i). If failed to 'Accept Terms and Conditions' when you register with the newly created account(s), you can accept the terms again via the Refresh Subscription Pools Tab.

  ii).All products with Unlimited Product of 'True' will create unlimited quantity subscriptions. For entitlement purposes the subscription and pool quantity for those products is unlimited.
